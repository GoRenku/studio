# Muted Earth, Stone, Bronze, And Textile Palette

Use a muted material palette grounded in stone, wool, leather, linen, bronze,
smoke, mud, parchment, and sea haze.

Accents should feel historical and worn rather than saturated or decorative.
Faded burgundy, tarnished gold, and oxidized bronze can draw attention, but they
should never turn the frame into fantasy spectacle.

## Avoid

- saturated modern blues
- clean digital white
- candy colors
- polished fantasy gold
