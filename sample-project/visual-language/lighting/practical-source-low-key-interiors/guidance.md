# Practical-Source Low-Key Interiors

Interior light should usually be motivated by practical historical sources:
candles, oil lamps, torches, furnace glow, or controlled window spill.

Shadows should be deep and brown. Highlights should feel warm, physical, and
localized. The viewer should understand where the light is coming from.

## Use When

- council rooms
- churches
- foundries at night
- private strategy scenes

## Avoid

- modern LED light
- blue fill
- glossy studio lighting
- clean commercial beauty light
