# Fifteenth-Century Ottoman And Byzantine Material Worlds

The world should be historically grounded in fifteenth-century Ottoman and
Byzantine material culture without turning into fantasy medieval imagery.

Props, costumes, buildings, tools, maps, documents, lamps, textiles, and weapons
should feel handmade, worn, and specific.

## Avoid

- fantasy armor
- Renaissance plate armor
- modern seams
- plastic props
- generic medieval village imagery
